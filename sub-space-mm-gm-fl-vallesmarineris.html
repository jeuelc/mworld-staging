Valles Marineris

 

The Valles Marineris, or Valley of the Mariners, is the largest canyon in the Solar System.

It is 4000 kilometres in length and up to 200 kilometres wide. It's so big that you can fit the whole of Japan in there, twice! It is up to 200 kilometres wide and up to 7 kilometres deep! It dwarfs anything similar on Earth.

How did such a vast valley form? Perhaps two parts of Mars' crust pulled apart from each other, producing a rift valley. There are rift valleys on Earth, but none is as spectacular as the Valles Mariner.

In 2010 something happened that shows even relatively small volcanoes can suddenly change how we live. It was the EyjafjallajÖkull volcano eruption in Iceland. The eruption started with lava flows and then went explosive! The earth shook and the glacier sitting on the top of the volcano began to melt and water rushed down its sides. Then a huge eruption column full of ash rose kilometres into the sky!

Although there was lots going on with this eruption, the main thing that made the world ask "what's the go with this Icelandic volcano?" was the impact of its eruption cloud. It was so thick and widespread it stopped aeroplane travel near Iceland and over Europe for a week! The stranded airport passengers had to just sleep at the airport! Imagine that no comfy bed, just hard plastic chairs and sharing the bathroom with thousands of other people for days on end!

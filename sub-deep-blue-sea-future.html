The future

The world's oceans cover 71% of the earth's surface and are vital in supporting the world's plants and animals. They provide food to many of the world's people, and support fishing and related industries around the world. The health of the world's oceans is essential to the health of the Earth as a whole and to you and me, our friends and our families.

Changes in the world's climate threaten the health of the oceans. It could also mean that the level of the world's oceans rise, threatening coastal communities around the world not to mention swamping some beautiful beaches!

There is also a lot of rubbish in the world's oceans, which hurts marine animals and has a big impact on our beautiful natural environment!

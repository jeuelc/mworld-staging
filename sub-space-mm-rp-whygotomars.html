Why go to Mars?

 

Why would you go to Mars?

Scientists want to go to Mars because it is the planet in our Solar System that most resembles Earth. Venus may be similar in size to the Earth, but its surface is far too hot for us to visit. The Moon is our closest neighbour, but the Moon never had a thick atmosphere and it never had running liquid water.

Mars is perhaps the right size, shape and temperature to have had an atmosphere, liquid water, mountains and valleys, volcanoes and perhaps even life.

Can you think of any other convincing reasons to go to Mars?
